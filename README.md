<section id="tips">
  <h2>Tips & Guides</h2>
  <h3>Donut Storage Tips</h3>
  <ul>
    <p><li>Store donuts in an airtight container at room temperature to keep them soft and fresh.</li></p>
    <p><li>Avoid refrigeration unless necessary — cold air dries donuts and cracks the glaze.</li></p>
    <p><li>Place wax paper between layers of frosted or filled donuts to prevent sticking.</li></p>
    <p><li>For a quick refresh, microwave a plain donut for 8-10 seconds to bring back warmth.</li></p>
    <p><li>If you prefer a crisp texture, reheat donuts in the oven at low heat for a few minutes.</li></p>
    <p><li>Always consume cream-filled donuts within 1–2 days for best taste and safety.</li></p>
    <p><li>Freeze leftover donuts by wrapping them tightly; thaw and warm before serving.</li></p>
  </ul>
